About Me

I am a software development student, musician, and athlete, passionate about everything I do. My journey combines technology with creativity, always striving to push boundaries and learn new things.

Music plays a central role in my life, both electronically and with traditional instruments. The experience of listening to vinyl records has become almost spiritual for me, connecting technology with artistry.

Nature and physical activity are essential parts of my lifestyle. I enjoy hiking, spending time outdoors, and staying socially active. My love for animals occasionally leads me to dog walking adventures.

Professionally, I aim to become a successful mobile software developer while maintaining a balance with my creative pursuits. Travel and cultural exploration are significant aspirations, as I believe they broaden perspectives and inspire innovation.
