About Me

I am a software development student, musician, and athlete, and I am passionate about everything I do.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I am an avid sports enthusiast and musician, both electronically and with traditional instruments. I love nothing more than losing myself in the sound of a vinyl record player, and I find the experience to be almost spiritual.

Being in nature is also a favorite pastime, and I am highly socially active. Animals are an important part of my life, and I sometimes have the opportunity to take a dog for a walk.

My ultimate goal is to be happy and proud of myself, and I am constantly striving towards this end. In my professional life, I aim to become a successful software developer, with a focus on the mobile industry. Along with my professional aspirations, I have a strong desire to explore different cultures and see the world. I also have an interest in spiritualism and find that music, nature, and exploring different cultures are all ways to connect with something greater than myself.
