My Hobbies

As a software development student, a lot of people think that i nearly have no free time (which is kinda true), but i somehow manage to do a lot of things in my free time.

Music Production

The process of creating music, something that has never been there before, is one of the, in my opinion, most beautiful things in the world.
It requires a reasonable amount of time to be able to produce a song. Depending on the genre, it can take a little more or less time, until the point is reached.
When i ask people what they think is the hardest part in music production, a lot of people think it's the arrangement
or maybe the mixing of all the different sounds into one big piece, but i would say it's definitely the ear you listen with.
What i mean by that is, you have to know when the mix of the song actually sounds good. It requires a lot of time and experience to know
how a good mix sounds.

Playing Guitar

Parallel to my studying start, i started playing the guitar. So i've been playing since September 2022
and i'm making quite good progress so far. I own two electric guitars, one acoustic and a ukulele. The one on the image was my first electric guitar, it's the Harley Benton TE-62CC.
The main reason why i started playing guitar was, so that i can add something new to my music production workflow.
This is very useful, since i can connect an electric guitar simply to my audio interface, record and use directly the riffs that i play on the guitar.
